Sonic Wind (LVL 1)
Sonic summons a large tornado and spins around it for a few seconds. An easy way to end combos with high damage; in addition, the tornado also sucks in all opponents.
Loop Kick (LVL 1)
Sonic loops around for a second or two, then targets onto the opponent. This move is challenging to combo into but quite rewarding, as it gives a hard knockdown and a large chunk of a Lightspeed bar.
Sonic Boost (LVL 2)
Sonic boosts into the enemy at high speeds with a projectile invuln strike. Upon hit he summons a massive Sonic Wind and strikes the opponent to the ground, this resets the juggle points and allows for a full combo upon hit. This move also restands which allows for simple pickups.
Ring Burst (LVL 2)
Sonic turns into a supercharged ball and becomes controllable with directions for a few seconds in the air. Use this move to catch an opponent off guard using its special movement properties. Be aware that it does have a height limit, and if it is reached Sonic will exit the ball state.
