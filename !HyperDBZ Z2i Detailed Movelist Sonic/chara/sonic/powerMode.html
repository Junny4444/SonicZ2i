Activate Highspeed Mode
"Let's get this party started!"
Sonic puts on his Lightspeed Bracelet (not pictured here) and gains a few new abilities:
-Quickness Gauge: A new bar that fills up and allows you to do special actions.
-Lightspeed cancel: using some of his Quickness Gauge allows him to special cancel any move into a Lightspeed attack. Uses 1/3 of the bar for Lightspeed Attack and 1/6 of the bar for Lightspeed Dash.
-Gains half off meter usage on all of his Lightspeed attacks
-All Lightspeed attacks become much faster
-Gains an air Lightspeed attack
-Gets a more cocky taunt that fills the Quickness Gauge slowly.

Note: All attacks that once only filled the Lightspeed Gauge now also fill Quickness Gauge.
Air Lightspeed Attack
Sonic does a fast dash in the air, on hit it knocks the opponent up for a combo like the grounded version. Use this after moves such as 6MK for a highly damaging combo.
Gem UNLOCK
Sonic pulls out a gem and gains the ability to cycle through multiple abilities that he can use to gain an upper hand in battle. This is done by inputting the left and right directions and the options are as follows:
Blue Gem: Sonic gains an ability to boost his speed to the limit with 6LK+MK, allowing him to cross the screen in record time.
Green Gem: Sonic gains the ability to summon a massive green tornado using 2LK+MK, granting amazing okizeme and block pressure. Sonic also gains a double jump.
Yellow Gem: Sonic gains the ability to summon an Electric Shield using 2LK+MK. This grants him a hit of invincibility and pulls in rings from afar. If the rings are picked up he gains some HP.

White Gem: Sonic gains the ability to charge a Homing Attack in the air using 2LK+MK. If it is charged it will become unblockable. While Sonic is charging the Homing Attack, he can stop his charge midway by pressing HK.
Grand Slam (LVL 2)
Sonic does an invulnerable on startup parry. Upon contact with a move, Sonic will retaliate with a deadly strike. This sends the opponent into his finishers. Do note that when the move is cancelled into on hit, the attack will start right away. This property grants Sonic the ability to combo into this super.
